Premium Australian Honey

Our bees roam pristine bush land on the South Coast and Southern Highlands of New South Wales. The pure and natural nectar they collect to make honey, mainly from Australian natives, is what makes up our range of gourmet honey.

100% Natural, Raw Honey

Our honey is not heat treated or processed in any way. Nothing artificial is added. We take the honey straight from the beehives and bottle it. Our batches are not blended together either so you get a delicious, pure flavour.

Special Honey, Special Use

Clayridge honey contain natural gluten, wheat, and fat-free, a perfect replacement for sugar. Our honey can be used on cereals, drinks or toast.
































Raw Honey Straight from Our Beehives

Clayridge Honey is a family owned, and family run, honey farm based on the South Coast of New South Wales. All of our honey is 100% pure, raw and completely natural. Our honey is harvested straight from the beehives ensuring fresh and high quality produce every time. Nothing artificial is added to Clayridge Honey. We do not use heat treatment nor do we pasteurise. Most importantly, all our honey is chemical free. 



Our bees collect pure and natural nectar from the untouched bushland of Jervis Bay. Therefore, the flavours or varieties offered by us are dependent on what is flowering. Much of our gourmet range is based on Australian natives including Bloodwood, Spotted Gum and Ironbark. Clayridge Honey want to make it easy for you to enjoy our honey so we offer free delivery, on orders $45 and over, into metro areas for NSW, VIC, QLD and ACT. We are confident you will be impressed by our delicious, pure gourmet honey.
